🚗 Car Insurance Analytics Dashboard – Power BI
📌 Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution built in Power BI to analyze car insurance performance across financial metrics, demographics, vehicle types, and geographic distribution.

The dashboard demonstrates:

- Data modeling
- DAX measure creation
- Interactive reporting
- KPI design
- Risk segmentation analysis

📊 Key Insights Delivered

- $111M Premiums Earned vs $19M Claims Paid
- $92M Underwriting Profit
- Highest claim volume from 65+ age group
- Certain vehicle brands showed higher claim frequency
- Geographic concentration of claims across specific U.S. states

🎯 Business Impact
This dashboard enables insurance stakeholders to:

- Monitor profitability
- Identify high-risk customer segments
- Optimize pricing strategies
- Detect geographic claim concentration
- Improve underwriting decisions
