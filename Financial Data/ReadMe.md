📊 **Financial Analysis Dashboard – Overview**

This project presents an interactive Financial Performance Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI and the Financial Sample dataset. The dashboard provides a clear overview of sales performance, profitability, discount patterns, product performance, and regional trends to support better business decision-making.

📌 **What This Dashboard Highlights**

📈 Sales & Profit Performance
Tracks key financial KPIs such as Sales, Orders, Profit, Profit Margin, and Discounts to evaluate overall business growth.

🌍 Regional Performance
Analyzes orders and profit margins by country to identify the most profitable markets and regions with growth potential.

🛍 Product Performance
Highlights top-performing products by sales, helping identify products that drive the majority of revenue.

💰 Discount Strategy Analysis
Visualizes how discounts are distributed across transactions and how they may affect overall profitability.

🏢 Customer Segment Profitability
Evaluates profit margins across different business segments to understand which customer groups contribute most to profits.

📅 Sales Trend Over Time
Displays monthly sales patterns, helping identify fluctuations, trends, and potential seasonal demand.

**🔍 Key Findings**

📊 Sales increased by 249% year-over-year, indicating strong business expansion.

💵 Profit grew significantly, reaching over 13M compared to 3.8M in the previous year.

⚠️ Profit margin slightly decreased, likely due to higher discount levels.

🌍 Germany and France show the strongest profit margins, while the United States has the lowest margin.

🏆 Paseo is the top-selling product, contributing the largest portion of revenue.

💸 Over 57% of sales involve high discounts, which may be impacting profitability.

**🚀 Business Impact**

Helps identify high-performing markets and products for strategic focus.

Reveals segments with low profitability, enabling corrective business strategies.

Provides insights into discount strategies affecting profit margins.

Supports data-driven decision making for pricing, sales strategy, and market expansion.
